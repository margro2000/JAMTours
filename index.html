JamTours

Authentic College Tours
By Real College Students

College Tours Made Personal

The current system of college touring is often inauthentic, impersonal, and uniformative. To solve this problem, the JAMTours team had the idea for an app that would connect prospective college students with current college students looking to give tours.

Learn more

Intuitive and Hassle Free

The steps are very simple: you sign up for an account (which would be different if you are a tour guide vs a prospective student). Then you fill out a brief questionnaire with your intended major, passions, and what you are looking for in a tour and tour guide. The app will then match you with students at the respective school who will best meet your tour preferences. With this process, tours will be best individualized for students, so students can get the best depiction of how their college experience might turn out
