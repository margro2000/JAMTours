Our Goal

The current system of college touring is inauthentic, impersonal, and uniformative.

To address this problem, the JAMTours team had the idea of an app which connects prospective college students with current college students looking to give tours.

The steps are simple:

1.

Sign up for an account

2.

Fill out a brief questionnaire about your intended major, passions, and what you're looking for in a tour and tour guide

3.

The app will match you with students at the respective school that best match your tour preferences
